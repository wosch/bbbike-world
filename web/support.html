BBBike professional plans

The BBBike extracts pro service also allows you to extract larger areas up to 48,000,000 square km and bigger files up to 3GB. You can get an API access for a large number of extracts. Support is available by email, messenger or phone.

Due the higher system load, we cannot offer this service for free.

The BBBike extract pro service starts at 99 Euro/month, depending on the number of extracts, bandwidth usage and support level. Feel free to contact us if you want use the extract pro service.
