This download site offers a database dump of the OpenStreetMap Full Planet in Protocolbuffer Binary Format. Also available are extracts of more than 200 cities and regions world wide in different formats.

  1. Download a ready extract of more than 200 cities and regions world wide, 2-50MB in size for each city. Supported formats are: OSM, PBF, Garmin map (osm, cycle, leisure style), Osmand, mapsforge, Navit, maps.me, SVG and Esri shapefile.

  2. Didn't find the area you want? Select your own region - a rectangle or polygon up to 6000 x 4000km large, or 768MB file size.

  3. For experts only: download the full planet, in PBF (25GB) format


All Data from OpenStreetMap is licensed under the OpenStreetMap License. Excerpts on this page are created and updated by BBBike.org. All times are given as UTC. The files will be updated once a week on Thursday evening.
